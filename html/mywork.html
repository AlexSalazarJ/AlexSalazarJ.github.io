Garmin

My second job in the tech industry was at Garmin as a Software Engineering Intern. I worked in their marine department over in Newport, OR. I worked on their autopilot displays and other various marine devices, which was all written in C. I also got to work with a CAN (Controller Area Network) bus datalogger and got experience by writing a script to translate the messages from hexadecimal to human readable english. The photo above is of the Marina in Nerport, OR. I was on the back of one of Garmin's boats when we went out to collect some data on the water.
